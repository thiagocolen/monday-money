Getting Started

MondayMoney is currently distributed as a Portable Windows Application.

Installation

  1. Download the latest MondayMoney.exe from the Releases page.
  2. Move it to a dedicated folder (e.g., Documents/MondayMoney).
  3. Run the executable.

Importing Data

To bring your financial history into MondayMoney:

  • Navigate to the Import page.
  • Select your bank source (Nubank, Binance, etc.).
  • Drag and drop your CSV file.
  • Confirm the detected transactions to add them to your ledger.

Managing Your Data

Your data is stored in the core/ folder created next to the executable. Simply back up this folder to keep your data safe.

Data belongs to the universe, but your CSVs belong to you.
