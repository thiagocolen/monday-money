Core Features

MondayMoney provides a comprehensive set of features to manage your personal finances without compromising your privacy.

Data Visualization

Interactive charts powered by Recharts allow you to understand your spending habits at a glance. You can view monthly spending trends, category breakdowns, and yearly comparisons.

CSV Integration

Easily import and manage transaction history using standard formats. We support various bank formats like Nubank and Binance, and provide tools to map generic CSVs to the MondayMoney ledger.

Category & Tag Management

Organize your transactions with customizable categories and tags. Use smart rules for auto-categorization and bulk tagging to manage large numbers of transactions efficiently.

Privacy & Security

All data is stored locally in core/data/ as CSV files. The application makes no external network calls, ensuring your financial information never leaves your machine.

Integrity Checks

Built-in validation tools ensure your ledger remains consistent, checking for duplicate transactions or data corruption automatically.

Data belongs to the universe, but your CSVs belong to you.
